Section 1

Mary Kate Castellani at Bloomsbury has acquired, in a pre-empt, debut author Charlotte Sullivan Wild's picture book Inside This Apple, a celebration of the big potential in small living things and especially in every child. Mary Lundquist (Cat & Bunny; Bloom) will illustrate. Publication is scheduled for spring 2019. Minju Chang of BookStop Literary Agency represented the author and Steven Malk of Writers House represented the illustrator.
