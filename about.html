Charlotte Sullivan Wild

Section 1

Charlotte Sullivan Wild has been telling stories since before she could even write. She dictated her first published story to her kindergarten teacher, Ms. Connors, who typed it on a typewriter. She moved on adventure and fantasy stories in grade school and now writes picture books. She loves walking through nature, kyaking, hiking, singing, rainbow spotting and eating chocolate. For many years she taught writing and stories in school. Now she gets to write her own stories all the time.

Professional bio:

Charlotte Sullivan Wild is the author of the forthcoming picture book INSIDE THIS APPLE, to be illustrated by Mary Lundquist (Bloomsbury, 2019). She is the recipient of grants from the Minnesota State Arts Board, the Metro Regional Arts Council, and The Jerome Foundation, as well as a 2012 Loft Shabo Picture book Award and a Pushcart nomination. She earned her MFA in fiction from Hamline University and has taught childrenâ€™s literature in the public schools and at the undergraduate level for nearly two decades. She has interviewed children's authors for Write On!, a program on KFAI Radio and regularly develops community programming related to children's literature. Originally from Minnesota, she now lives in sunny San Antonio, TX, where she hosts The Picture Book Salon of San Antonio.
