the writing community

Stories are most delicious when shared â€“ at bedtime, on the playground, at work, or curled up in a quiet corner with hot cocoa. The process of telling stories is no different. It takes a village!

I could never do what I do as an artist without the patience, feedback, kind encouragement, and lively discussions with fellow artists. When I first dreamt of being a writer, I had no idea that dream would lead me to one of the kindest and most engaged communities around.

I have been a part of two wonderful writing communities, one in Minnesota and one in Texas. Here are some of the great community programs in Minnesota, Texas and beyond.

Welcome to our village. Enjoy conversations about the world of children's books and making art. Find a place to connect with other writers. Be inspired to build community where you are. Most of all, welcome. I'm glad you're here.

Minnesota community

  • The Loft Literary Center has great classes on children's literature and other kinds of writing.
  • Molly Beth Griffin hosts a Picture Book Salon where they meet once a month to discuss all things picture books. Molly's PB Salon inspired me to start one in Texas!
  • Write On! Radio, a program on KFAI Radio, where they talk writing with writers.
