the writing community

When people think of writing, they think of someone sitting alone at a desk. But it's so much more than that! Writers depend on a whole community of resources. Where communities are strong, writers thrive.

I have been lucky enough to be a part of two wonderful writing communities, one in Minnesota and one in Texas. Here are some of the great community programs in Minnesota, Texas and beyond.

I have interviewed children's authors for Write On!, a program on KFAI Radio. I regularly develop community programming related to children's literature. Originally from Minnesota, I now live in sunny San Antonio, TX, where I host The Picture Book Salon of San Antonio.

Minnesota community

  • The Loft Literary Center has great classes on children's literature. I sometimes teach classes here.
  • Molly Beth Griffin hosts a Picture Book Salon where they meet once a month to discuss all things picture books. Molly's PB Salon inspired me to start one in Texas!
  • Write On! Radio, a program on KFAI Radio.
